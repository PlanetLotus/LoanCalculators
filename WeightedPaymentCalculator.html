Weighted Payment Calculator

When you have multiple loans that you want to pay down faster, you need to know how to minimize the amount of interest generated. This means always focusing your payments on the loans that generate the most interest and making the minimum payments on any other loans that don't meet this criteria. Here you can put the principal and interest rate of as many loans as you wish and this page will tell you what to focus on.
